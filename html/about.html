Adaptively Versatile

In the last four years, I have lived in eight different cities in four states, which has taught me how to adapt to new situations. Some people hate change, but I love it because I believe new environments and circumstances provide opportunities for growth and learning. I enjoy pushing myself to learn new things and can normally pick up a new skill if given a proper chance (unless it requires advanced levels of hand-eye coordination - in which case, stand back). The opportunities I've had to frequently experience new situations has allowed me to develop a wide variety of skills that make me capable of analyzing, strategizing, and executing in creative ways.

Simply Candid

I believe in being 100% honest at all times. As a society we lose precious time and resources by sugar-coating our communications and telling little lies to avoid confrontation, consequences, and offense. (If the dress doesn't look good, tell her now. She'll thank you later when she wants to post on Instagram). Candor provides opportunities for open communication, sharing of diverse opinions, and development of creative ideas by encouraging open conversation. Simple candor offers clarity.
